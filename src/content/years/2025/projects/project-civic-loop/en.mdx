Project Civic Loop establishes a structured feedback mechanism between research teams and municipal administrative units. Quarterly transfer sessions, a shared needs register, and a digital collaboration space ensure that research questions are derived directly from administrative problem contexts. In 2025, 23 municipalities were involved and 12 concrete research mandates were formulated, four of which have already resulted in ongoing projects. The model is referenced as a benchmark for evidence-based municipal administration within the GovTech network.
