Project Horizon Grid builds a modular research infrastructure providing adaptive modelling pipelines for heterogeneous data sources. The architecture allows research groups to combine standardised components and operate them on institutional or cloud infrastructure without requiring deep DevOps expertise. In the 2025 rollout year, 14 research groups across six institutions were onboarded; the platform processed more than one billion data points per quarter for the first time. Further integrations with European research infrastructures such as EOSC are planned by end of 2025.
