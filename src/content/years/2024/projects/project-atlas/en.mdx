Project Atlas builds a coordinated data infrastructure that brings together regional climate and health data from six European partner regions. Integration of heterogeneous data sources — from municipal health registers to Copernicus satellite data — is achieved through standardised interfaces and a shared ontological model. In the 2024 scaling year, the platform was extended to three additional regions and a joint analysis publication with all consortium partners was submitted for the first time. The architecture is intended as a European standard for networked climate health research.
